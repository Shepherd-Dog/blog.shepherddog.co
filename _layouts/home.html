What's Your Core Competency?

Is it roasting coffee? Baking cakes? Caring for the elderly or infirm? Building devices that connect to the internet? Providing educational resources?

Ours is building and maintaining software tools for businesses — tools used by your customers and employees. Our Strengths

It's hard to boil a company (even a small company like ours) down into three strengths but this layout looks good and we don't want to overwhelm you. In addition to these, you can read about our core values or some of our blog posts.

  • Size

    We're a small, focused team. Our size allows us to avoid communication and project management overhead. Our diverse skillsets allow us to handle everything needed to build and maintain software from initial discovery to final testing.

    We are technology-agnostic, speak many languages, and have experience with a variety of frameworks. This allows us to choose the right thing for your project.
